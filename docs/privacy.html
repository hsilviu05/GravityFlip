Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Gravity Flip: Arcade ("the App") is developed by Ionut-Silviu Hermeneanu ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

Data We Collect Directly

The App stores the following data locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us:

  • Your best distance score
  • Total coins collected
  • Sound and haptics preferences
  • Remove Ads purchase status

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use the following data to serve ads:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Device type, OS version, and app interaction data

If you deny the tracking permission, AdMob will serve non-personalised ads and will not use your IDFA. You can change this at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information on how Google uses data, see Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

Remove Ads (In-App Purchase)

If you purchase Remove Ads, banner and interstitial ads are permanently disabled. Rewarded ads remain available by your choice. The purchase is handled entirely by Apple's StoreKit — we do not receive or store your payment information.

Game Center

If you are signed into Game Center, your best score is submitted to the Gravity Flip leaderboard. This is governed by Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top will always reflect the latest version.
