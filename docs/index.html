Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I flip gravity?
    Tap anywhere on the screen. Your character will arc to the opposite surface.
  • Why can't I flip mid-air?
    That's the core mechanic — you must land before you can flip again.
  • How do I continue a run after dying?
    On the Game Over screen, tap Continue to watch a short rewarded ad and resume.
  • What does Remove Ads include?
    It removes banner ads and interstitials permanently. Rewarded ads (Continue / Double Coins) remain available by choice.
  • How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
    Open Settings inside the game and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Does the game work offline?
    Yes — gameplay, scores, and settings all work with no internet connection.
